# v0.9.0 - News & Blog System

**Released:** 2026-03-18

v0.9.0 introduces a built-in news and blog system, bringing patch notes, announcements, and dev updates directly into the site.

---

## News Drawer

Good news! A NEW **News** panel now lives on the right side of the site. It slides in with a dimmed backdrop and can be opened at any time via the NEW **News** button in the header.
*new new new new new new new News.*

- **Auto-opens for new posts** - The drawer automatically opens when a new post has been published since your last visit. Once closed, it won't open again until another post is added.
- **Hash routing** - Posts are deep-linkable via `#news` and `#news/<post-id>` in the URL, so specific posts can be shared directly.

---

## Markdown-Based Posts

Posts are written in **Markdown** and rendered via [marked.js](https://marked.js.org), supporting the full range of formatting you'd expect:

- Headings, bold, italic, strikethrough
- Ordered and unordered lists
- Inline code and fenced code blocks with syntax highlighting
- Tables
- Blockquotes
- Images and animated GIFs
- Embedded HTML5 video
- Raw HTML blocks for custom layouts

---

## Localization Support

Each post can optionally provide a Chinese (`zh`) variant. When the site language is set to Chinese, the localized version is served automatically with no extra steps for the reader.
