# v0.9.1 - News System Polish

**Released:** 2026-03-18

v0.9.1 is a polish pass on the news system introduced in v0.9.0, adding animated transitions, card thumbnails, hero media banners, and styled tags.

---

## Animated Page Transitions

Navigating between the post list and individual posts now animates smoothly.

---

## News Card Thumbnails

Post cards in the list now show a thumbnail on the right side:

- Posts with a `title_media` field display that image or video as a card thumbnail.
- `patch-notes` posts automatically use the EFTForge site logo if no custom media is set.
- GIF & video thumbnails are rendered as a frozen first frame to keep the list performant.

---

## Post Hero Banner

When opening a post, the `title_media` (image, GIF, or video) is rendered as a cinematic hero banner at the top of the post, with the post title overlaid on top via a gradient. Patch-notes posts without custom media fall back to the site logo.

---

## Styled Tag Variants

Tags on cards and post headers now have distinct styles based on their type.

---

## Other News Improvements

- Videos embedded in posts default to **autoplay, loop, muted** with no controls. Add `data-controls="true"` on the video element to opt back in.
- All hyperlinks in post content now open in a **new tab**.
- The "new post" auto-open check now correctly ignores dev-only posts — closing the drawer on localhost will no longer mark the dev post as seen.
- Added `overflow-x: hidden` to the news panel body to prevent horizontal scrollbar on narrow viewports.
