# v1.1.10 - UI Overhaul, Accuracy Stat & Tracker Improvements

**Released:** 2026-04-20

v1.1.10 is a substantial visual and **accuracy** update, the biggest UI rework since... the last UI rework!... The background, header, and gun cards have all been reworked, and most important of all: This update introduces the **Accuracy** stat calculation.

---

## New Background

The site now has a fully dynamic background! A subtle dot grid shifts with your mouse cursor for a parallax depth effect. A new blob of golden glow accent in the background is added and reacts to what you're doing, it shifts color to match active notifications, and turns teal when compare mode / PvE mode in the price panel is on.

---

## Header Improvements

The header now expands and collapses in the gunSelect panel, on this screen it first opens up to show full nav button labels, a larger logo, and the site tagline, and collapses once you scroll down. Once you select a gun and enter the build view it compacts into icon-only mode to reclaim vertical space.

The header has been given the site gold gradient as well, and the header buttons have been given SVG icons when the header is collapsed!

Header buttons and language switcher hover states have also been updated to use a golden tint instead of plain grey to better blend with the new gradient.

---

## Gun Card Border Animation

Hovering over a gun card now triggers an animated conic-gradient border that sweeps in from the exact angle your mouse enters the card, and retracts cleanly on leave. The animation is disabled on mobile. This makes it clearer which gun your mouse is currently hovering over.

---

## Accuracy Stat

Accuracy is now fully calculated and displayed. Every weapon shows its **MOA** (minute of angle) value, derived from the base weapon's center of impact and any installed attachments that affect it.

- **MOA** measures how tight your shots group at distance. Lower is better, a lower MOA means your shots land closer together.
- **Attachment accuracy modifiers** are shown in the attachment table as a `+/-` percentage. A positive value tightens your grouping, a negative value opens it up. These are factored into the final MOA displayed on the stats panel.

---

## Stat Tracker - New Stats

The stat change tracker now monitors two new stats:

- **MOA (Center of Impact in backend)** - Tracks base weapon accuracy changes. Values are displayed as converted MOA.
- **Accuracy % (accuracy modifier)** - Tracks attachment accuracy modifier changes, displayed with a `+/-` sign and `%` suffix.
