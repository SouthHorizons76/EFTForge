# v1.0.2 - Community Builds QoL Improvements

**Released:** 2026-03-26

v1.0.2 is a focused update on the community builds experience. The per-gun build limit has been raised from 50 to 500, and the Explore Builds modal has been improved with search, sorting, and a wider layout to make browsing more practical.

---

## 500 Builds Per Gun

The previous cap of 50 community builds per gun was too low, popular weapons like the M4A1 were filling up fast and newer builds were hardly visible. The limit is now **500 per gun**.

Once a gun reaches 500 builds, new publishes for that gun will be rejected with an error message. If this ever becomes a problem, please reach out to me directly!

---

## Search in Community Builds

A search bar now sits above the community build cards. Start typing to filter by build name in real time!

---

## Sort Dropdown

Builds can now be sorted by:

- **Featured** - default view, featured builds pinned to the top
- **Newest** - most recently published first
- **Most Loaded** - builds that have been loaded the most times
- **Highest Rated** - builds with the most likes
- **Highest EED** - sorted by EvoErgoDelta, descending
- **Best V.Recoil** - lowest vertical recoil first
- **Cheapest** - sorted by live cost

---

## Wider Modal, 3-Column Grid

The Explore Builds modal has been widened to 820px to make room for a **3-card-per-row** grid. The builds list area is also taller to fit more without scrolling. On mobile the grid stays at 2 columns.
